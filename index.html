About Me

It all started with a ferocious blizzard during a winter break. Stuck in my house with nothing to do, I randomly decided to go through some coding tutorials to relieve my boredom.

It was fun. Enough so that when the blizzard subsided and I was given the choice to leave the house, I turned it down and continued to code.

I taught myself HTML, CSS, Javascript, and popular frameworks/libraries like Node and React. This website serves as a convenient place for me to demonstrate what I have created and what my next goals are.

I hope you enjoy!
